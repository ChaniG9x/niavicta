Real-Time Bedside Notes

Capture vital signs, medications, and observations directly at the point of care.

Secure Sync

All entries sync to the central record system (EHR or EMR), helping reduce duplication, errors, and time spent documenting.

On-the-Ward Usability

We are shaping every screen and button based on how nurses actually work - even during shift changes, emergencies, or power cuts.

Training & Facilitation Included

Simple onboarding supported by local facilitators who understand the workflow, with hands-on guidance to get started with confidence.
