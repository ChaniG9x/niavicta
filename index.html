We understand the pressure nurses face

We’ve seen how documentation burden leads to burnout and errors. That’s why we’re designing the Bedside Documentation Device (BDD) in close collaboration with healthcare workers – to cut documentation time in half, improve record accuracy, and give nurses more time for patients.

Currently in early development – seeking user needs research sites and funding support.

How the BDD will work in your hospital

  1. Install the BDD at every bedside.
  2. Train staff in under 30 minutes.
  3. Capture accurate patient records instantly, without leaving the patient’s side.
